# Executive Report Review Checklist

**Document ID:** LHT-Governance-Checklist-004

---

# Purpose

This checklist ensures executive reports are technically accurate, evidence-backed, and suitable for leadership review.

---

# Executive Summary

- [ ] Objectives clearly stated.
- [ ] Scope accurately described.
- [ ] Overall risk posture summarized.
- [ ] Business impact communicated.

---

# Findings

- [ ] Critical findings included.
- [ ] High-risk findings prioritized.
- [ ] Risk ratings verified.
- [ ] Evidence references included.

---

# Detection Coverage

- [ ] Detection effectiveness summarized.
- [ ] Coverage gaps identified.
- [ ] MITRE ATT&CK mapping verified.
- [ ] Purple Team outcomes included.

---

# Remediation

- [ ] Prioritized roadmap included.
- [ ] Exposure reduction explained.
- [ ] Owners assigned.
- [ ] Validation strategy documented.

---

# Governance

- [ ] Evidence claims verified.
- [ ] No unsupported statements.
- [ ] Naming conventions followed.
- [ ] Report version updated.

---

# Final Quality Review

- [ ] Grammar reviewed.
- [ ] Formatting verified.
- [ ] Figures referenced.
- [ ] Screenshots validated.
- [ ] Executive presentation aligned.
